Games

The minigames are mostly based on the PVP skills, bowing skills and breaking skills. The games are:

Forest fight: It is a game where the players are placed in a custom map, with moss walls all over the ground. The players fight each other with only swords.

Hot potato: A random player will be given an item called hot potato. The item depletes the health of the player. He/She can throw it to other people to make them lose their health. The one who survives wins.

Spleef: The players will be placed on a floor with blastproof ceramic. The players will be given a diamond pickaxe. They can kill others by breaking blocks and making them fall. The one who survives him.

Bowminton: Players will be in a badminton-like court and will be split into teams. One team cannot enter the court of the other team and vice versa. There will a net in the middle, blocking the players of one team to enter the court of the other team. There will be a hole in the net through which a team can shoot the other team through bows and arrows.
